# ROPA Template (Draft)

A Records of Processing Activities (ROPA) entry helps document the core data processing operations within an organization, as required under GDPR Article 30. This template provides a repeatable structure for capturing essential information about each processing activity.

---

## 1. Processing Activity Name
Example: User Account Management, Customer Support Tickets, Marketing Email Distribution

---

## 2. Purpose of Processing
Describe the primary reason this activity occurs:
- Account creation
- Authentication
- Support case tracking
- Analytics
- Fraud prevention

---

## 3. Categories of Data Subjects
List the people whose data is involved:
- Customers
- Website visitors
- Employees
- Vendors or partners

---

## 4. Categories of Personal Data
Examples:
- Identification data (name, email, phone)
- Authentication data (password hashes)
- Behavioral/usage data
- Support ticket content
- Device/IP information

---

## 5. Data Recipients
List internal and external receivers:
- Internal teams (Support, Security, Engineering)
- Third-party processors (cloud hosting, analytics vendors)

---

## 6. Transfers to Third Countries
If applicable:
- Countries involved
- Safeguards (SCCs, adequacy decision, encryption in transit)

---

## 7. Retention Periods
Provide defined retention timelines:
- 30 days for logs
- 1 year for tickets
- As long as the contract is active

---

## 8. Technical and Organizational Controls
Examples:
- Role-based access controls
- Encryption at rest/in transit
- Logging and monitoring
- Data minimization practices

---

## 9. DPIA Required?
Indicate whether this activity triggers a Data Protection Impact Assessment.
