# DPIA Template (Draft)

Use this template to document a Data Protection Impact Assessment in a structured, repeatable way.

---

## 1. Context and Scope
Describe:
- The project or system
- Data flows involved
- Objectives and stakeholders

---

## 2. Data Inventory
List:
- Data types collected
- Processing purposes
- Storage locations
- Retention periods

---

## 3. Lawful Basis Evaluation
Document the lawful basis for each processing purpose, such as:
- Consent
- Contract
- Legal obligation
- Legitimate interest

---

## 4. Risk Analysis
Identify risks related to:
- Confidentiality
- Integrity
- Availability
- Rights and freedoms of data subjects

---

## 5. Controls and Mitigations
Describe the technical and organizational controls that reduce the identified risks.

---

## 6. DPIA Summary & Approval
Provide:
- Final risk rating
- Recommendations
- Reviewer name and date
- Approval signature (if applicable)
